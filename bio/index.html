NRF & Public-Private Partnership

When he formally joined the NRF in 2005, LaRocca became the face of the industry‚Äôs fight against Organized Retail Crime (ORC) and e-Fencing. He used newly created research, media relationships, and legislative pressure to collaborate with law enforcement partners globally.

Recognition

In 2006, he was awarded the Chain Store Age "40 Under 40" Award. Between 2005 and 2010, the FBI recognized him three times for outstanding assistance to the Bureau and exceptional service in the interest of public safety.

During his career, he passionately supported the creation of recognition programs, including the Law Enforcement Retail Partnership Award and the NRF Ring of Excellence Award.

Volunteer Experience

LaRocca served as a Volunteer Reserve with the Los Angeles Police Department. He is a Founding Member of the Los Angeles Area Organized Retail Crime Association (LAAORCA) and a former advisor to the Southern California Chapter of the IAFCI.
