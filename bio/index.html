Full Biography

Nationally recognized leader in loss prevention, organized retail crime, and emerging technologies.

Background & Experience

An innovative asset protection/security professional with over 25 years of experience, Joseph LaRocca is a nationally recognized leader and public speaker on loss prevention, organized retail crime and emerging technologies.

As the former Director of Loss Prevention for the Walt Disney Company’s Retail Division, Disney Stores, LaRocca directed strategic and tactical loss prevention, crisis management and business continuity programs. Internally these programs were consistently recognized by leadership and his team twice received the President’s Award and the Team Excellence Award for improving profits and maintaining revenue streams during crisis events.

In 1998, he created LPInformation.com, which grew to become one of the top industry networking sites in the U.S. In 2005, the National Retail Federation acquired the system, which served as NRF’s loss prevention portal for over a decade. LaRocca also contributed a dedicated column to NRF’s STORES Magazine for several years.

Industry Leadership

While at the NRF, LaRocca was the face of the industry’s fight against Organized Retail Crime (ORC) and e-Fencing. He collaborated with retailers and law enforcement partners to leverage industry research, media relationships, and legislative pressure at both state and federal levels.

In partnership with the U.S. Department of Homeland Security, he initiated the publication of the national Active Shooter program and was a founding member of the DHS Commercial Facilities Sector Coordinating Council. He successfully obtained Secret clearances and HSIN.gov/LEO.gov access for key retail executives and co-founded the S.E.A.R.C.H. program with U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE-HSI).

Legislative Testimony & Media

LaRocca has testified multiple times before the U.S. Congress, House of Representatives, regarding cyber-security, consumer data protection, and the Organized Retail Crime Act. At the state level, his testimony has informed legislation in California, Maryland, Colorado, and Vermont, often in conjunction with ALEC and NCSL.

As an industry spokesperson, he has conducted hundreds of television, print, and radio interviews. He frequently assists producers and writers in analyzing the critical details of retail loss prevention issues for long-format television segments and trade publications.

Recognition & Awards

In 2006, LaRocca received the Chain Store Age "40 Under 40" Award. On multiple occasions (2005, 2006, and 2010), the FBI recognized him for outstanding assistance and exceptional service in the interest of public safety. He has also championed the creation of industry honors, including the Law Enforcement Retail Partnership Award and the Ring of Excellence Award.

Volunteer Experience

LaRocca served as a Volunteer Reserve with the Los Angeles Police Department and is a Founding Member of the Los Angeles Area Organized Retail Crime Association (LAAORCA). He is a former Advisor to the Southern California Chapter of the International Association of Financial Crimes Investigators (IAFCI).
