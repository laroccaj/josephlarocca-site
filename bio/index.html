Expertise & History

Biography

Joseph LaRocca is a nationally recognized leader and public speaker on loss prevention, organized retail crime, and emerging technologies.

The Walt Disney Company & Early Career

Prior to his advisory roles, LaRocca spent nearly a decade as the Director of Loss Prevention for The Walt Disney Company’s Retail Division (Disney Stores). There, he directed strategic loss prevention, crisis management, and business continuity programs. His leadership earned his team the Disney President’s Award and the Team Excellence Award for protecting revenue during high-impact global events.

National Retail Federation (NRF)

In 2005, Joseph joined the National Retail Federation (NRF) as Vice President and Senior Advisor of Loss Prevention. He became the face of the industry’s fight against Organized Retail Crime (ORC). He was instrumental in building the NRF’s loss prevention industry portal and served as an intermediary between the nation's largest retailers and global law enforcement agencies.

Government Relations & Advocacy

LaRocca has provided expert testimony before the U.S. Congress on multiple occasions, specifically regarding the Organized Retail Crime Act and consumer data protection. He served as a founding member of the DHS Commercial Facilities Sector Coordinating Council and collaborated with U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE-HSI) and the FBI. Between 2005 and 2010, the FBI recognized him three times for exceptional service in the interest of public safety.

Community & Industry Recognition

A passionate advocate for the profession, Joe is a Founding Member of the Los Angeles Area Organized Retail Crime Association (LAAORCA) and a Volunteer Reserve with the Los Angeles Police Department. He is the recipient of the NRF Ring of Excellence Award, recognizing pioneers in the retail LP industry.
