Joseph LaRocca

Retail nerd · Crime Prevention Advocate · Kidney donor

Expert in Retail Asset Protection & Loss Prevention

With more than three decades of experience in retail asset protection and security, Joseph LaRocca is a recognized authority on loss prevention and organized retail crime. His career includes serving as Director of Loss Prevention for Disney Stores and the National Retail Federation.

Founder of RetaiLPartners

Today, as founder of RetaiLPartners, he advises retailers and organizations on effective loss prevention strategies, operational risk, and emerging technologies. He is also a nationally recognized speaker and media resource on retail crime trends.
