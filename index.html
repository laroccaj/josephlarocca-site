Joseph LaRocca

Retail nerd; Crime Prevention Advocate; TV/Film blur; Hockey fan; Kidney donor.

A nationally recognized leader in loss prevention, organized retail crime (ORC), and public-private sector partnerships. Formerly with The Walt Disney Company and the National Retail Federation.

Today, Joseph serves as a strategic advisor and industry spokesperson, bridging the gap between global retailers and law enforcement. A frequent contributor to major news outlets like CNN and Fox News, he leverages decades of experience to advocate for smarter security technologies and resilient business strategies. Whether in the boardroom or at a national summit, his mission remains centered on fostering collaboration to protect people and assets alike.
